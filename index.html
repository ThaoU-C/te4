This work was created as an NFT from a picture taken when Bi took Helen for a walk.
Dog ownership in the US carries an attractive aura that speaks beyond just having a pet.
Walking a dog through the neighborhood enhances the owner's image as friendly, active, and stable.
The artwork was painted in Photoshop to demonstrate the perception of the dog-owner relationship.
